Our Services

In pursuit of excellence, we require the best performance from our staff. We also coordinate care with other medical professionals to ensure that our clients are receiving the best care possible.

Medical Services

Skilled Nursing

Includes assisting the patient in managing the symptoms of chronic conditions, assessing and treating wounds, instructing the patient in mediation management and new or exacerbated diagnoses, teaching self administration of gastrostomy feeds, IM SQ injections, and drawing and monitoring lab values.

Physical Therapy

Includes therapeutic exercise, balance activities and ambulation training to regain functional mobility and gross motor skills.

Speech Therapy

Includes the treatment and management of voice disorders and special techniques to facilitate the patient's communication skills.

Occupational Therapy

Includes techniques for sensory integrations, energy conservation, joint protection, compensatory techniques to improve the level of independence in the activities of daily living, and therapeutic activities designed to restore physical function.

Non-Medical Care

Personal Services

Includes assistance with eating, grooming, dressing, personal hygiene, and mobility. We also provide home assessments and visits with a registered nurse, medication administration, diet and nutrition monitoring, incontinent care, and updates for your primary caretakers and physicians regarding your health.

Companion Care Services

Includes assistance with scheduling, communication, escorts to walks and appointments, entertainment, and more.

Home Helper Services

Includes light housekeeping, laundry, home organization, meal planning, pet care, and personal errands.

Patient Advocacy

Home Health Assistance

Home healthcare is an new and emerging branch of the health industry. Our assistants are dedicated to helping you find the right in-home care solution for you to meet your needs.

Medical Assistance

With confusing charts, technical jargon, and a sea of numbers, our medical professionals can help you understand your health through reviews of your test reports, medical records, appointments, and more.

Insurance and Financial Assistance

With an ever-expanding health insurance market, we can help you find the plan that is the best fit for you. We also can help you file claims, understand billing and costs, and other related issues.

Legal Assistance

We believe that our patients should only receive top-notch care. Our staff can help with medical error reviews, filing disability, worker's compensation, and malpractice should legal issues ever arise.
