About Us

We are a not-for-profit, community-based organization dedicated to providing comprehensive in-home healthcare services to help your maintain your independence.

Our Mission

Golden Pacific Home Health, Inc. is committed to optimizing your health and wellness with kindness and the help you need.

Our goal is to optimize your health, help coordinate your support systems of family, friends and neighbors and return you to health. We are available to help you sort out your needs no matter how complex.

Who We Care For

We provide care for those who need assistance with

  • infusion services
  • memory and mobility loss
  • declining independence
  • chronic illnesses and/or disabilities

We also provide care for anybody with a physician's referral to receive home healthcare services.
