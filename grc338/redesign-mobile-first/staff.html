Our Staff

Golden Pacific Home Health, Inc. has licensed professionally supervised clinicians and caregivers to assist you.

Our goal is to optimize your health, coordinate your support systems of family, friends and neighbors. We are available to assist you in sorting out your needs.

Our Caregivers

Our staff is selected not only for their knowledge, skills and experience, but also for their patient advocacy, dependability and the compassion needed to work with you and your loved ones. Our staff are...

  • professionally certified and licensed
  • matched to your preferences
  • trained and experienced
  • background and referenced checked
  • criminal and driving record checked
  • health-screened and TB tested
  • CPR and first-aid certified

Employment Opportunities

Looking for a career in healthcare? We're always looking for compassionnate, competent people for our team! Our hiring staff will review your work experience and skills set. Contact us for more information.
